# Barua Construction Website

## Brand Identity

**Brand name:** Barua Construction

### Brand Personality

- Trustworthy
- Professional
- Premium
- Reliable
- Modern
- Architecturally excellent

## Design Principles

1. Prioritize clarity over complexity.
2. Maintain premium spacing and typography.
3. Use high-quality architectural imagery.
4. Ensure strong mobile responsiveness.
5. Focus on lead generation and project discovery.
6. Create a luxury real estate experience.
7. Use subtle, purposeful animations.
8. Ensure every page builds credibility.

## UI Standards

- Use the official blue-teal to green brand gradient for primary accents.
- Use deep teal for trust-building emphasis and pale mint for soft surfaces.
- Avoid unrelated accent colors that compete with the official logo.
- Use large, visually engaging hero sections.
- Maintain generous whitespace and a clear visual hierarchy.
- Use premium card designs with elegant shadows.
- Apply glass effects only when they improve readability or depth.
- Use restrained, modern gradients.
- Use consistent corner radii between 12px and 16px.
- Follow the shared component and design-token system.
- Keep interactive states clear, accessible, and consistent.

## User Experience Goals

Visitors should immediately understand:

- Who Barua Construction is
- Which projects are available
- Why they should trust the company
- How to contact the company

## Conversion Goals

### Primary

- Contact form submissions

### Secondary

- Project enquiries
- Phone calls
- Brochure downloads

## Content Tone

Content should be:

- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Premium
- Aspirational
- Clear and concise

Avoid buzzwords, excessive marketing language, unsupported claims, and
cluttered layouts.

## Technical Standards

- SEO-first implementation
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Accessible markup and interactions
- Fast loading and optimized assets
- Reusable components
- Scalable architecture
- Data-driven project and company content

## Success Criteria

The website should feel comparable to a premium real estate developer and
appeal to serious home buyers, investors, and property seekers. Every design
and engineering decision should strengthen trust, simplify project discovery,
or make it easier for a qualified visitor to contact Barua Construction.
